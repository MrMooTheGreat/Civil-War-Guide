To the Gallant & Patriotic

You have answered the call of your country. By trading the plow for the musket, you have entered a world of rigid discipline and hardship. The path ahead is marked by the drumbeat of the drillmaster and the dust of the long march.

This guide serves to prepare the green recruit for the realities of the field—from the construction of a proper lean-to to the maintenance of your Springfield rifle. Carry it well, for knowledge is as vital as powder.
